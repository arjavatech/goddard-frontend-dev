Group Photos in Electronic Daily Activity Reports

The Goddard School takes photos of individual children and groups of children for electronic daily activity reports. These photos will not be used for any other purpose by The Goddard School or Goddard Systems, Inc. Although we have a School policy against it, it is possible for individuals who receive group photos in an electronic daily activity reports to share these group photos through their personal social media accounts. For this reason, we ask that you specifically authorize whether we can include your child in any group photos shared through the electronic daily activity reports.
